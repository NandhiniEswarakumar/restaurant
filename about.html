Our Story

Podhigai Thottam Resort was founded with a vision to blend luxury and nature. Nestled in the heart of scenic beauty, our resort offers a tranquil escape for families, couples, and solo travelers. Our mission is to provide an eco-friendly, comfortable, and memorable experience for every guest.

Established: 2020
Mission: Harmony with Nature
Values: Sustainability, Hospitality, Excellence
Vision: To be the leading eco-resort in South India, setting benchmarks in green hospitality.
Sustainability: 80% of our energy is from renewable sources, and we use organic farming for our kitchen.
Guest Experience: Personalized service, curated local experiences, and 24/7 support for every guest.
Awards: Winner of the 2024 Green Resort Excellence Award.
Community: We support local artisans and employ from nearby villages.
Commitment: Continuous improvement in comfort, safety, and eco-friendly practices.
